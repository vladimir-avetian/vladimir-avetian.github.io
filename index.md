I am a Postdoctoral Scholar at the [«Governance and Regulation» Chair](https://chairgovreg.fondation-dauphine.fr/fr/node/1) of the University Paris-Dauphine - PSL. 

I received my PhD in Economics from Sciences Po under supervision of [Sergeï Guriev](https://sites.google.com/site/sguriev/). 

*Research interests:* Political Economy, Urban Economics, Applied Micro.

*Research methods:* Causal Inference, Unstructured Data, Text-as-Data.

I organise ["Public Governance" online seminar](https://acss-dig.psl.eu/fr/seminaires/public-governance) focused on Political Economy + AI / Data Science. Please, contact me if you want to attend or present. 

**My CV is available [here](https://vladimir-avetian.github.io/pdfs/cv.pdf).**

Personal: I love the arts and sciences. I hate tyrants and wars, including Putin’s war in Ukraine.
